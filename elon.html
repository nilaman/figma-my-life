Interests

Elon Musk's interests span both his professional ventures and personal hobbies. As the CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, he is focused on space exploration, with a goal of making life multi-planetary, and advancing electric vehicles and renewable energy to combat climate change. His other companies, Neuralink and The Boring Company, explore AI-human integration and innovative transportation solutions.

Personally, Musk enjoys science fiction and fantasy, with books like The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy inspiring his vision for the future. He's also a fan of video games like Deus Ex and enjoys engaging with memes and social media, reflecting a playful side to his otherwise ambitious goals.
